## 2. Description of the App

My Gratitude Jar is a personal journaling and mindfulness application that allows users to record gratitude entries, reflect on memories, practise daily affirmations, and access breathing exercises. The App is available on the Google Play Store and is operated from South Africa.

The App includes a weather widget on the home screen that fetches local weather conditions using your device's location. Premium users also have access to AI-powered personalised journaling prompts and entry suggestions, generated using Google's Gemini API based on their selected mood and time of day.

The App offers a free tier and an optional premium subscription with additional features, processed via Google Play Billing and managed by RevenueCat.
